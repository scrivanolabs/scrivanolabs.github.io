A simple and easy to use app for taking handwritten notes on Windows 10/11.

Different backgrounds and page sizes

You can customise your documents by choosing between 4 different paper backgrounds: Plain, Lined, Grid or Dotted with customisable spacing. So you can export your notes as PDF documents in traditional printing formats.

Grid Snapping

In Scrivano you can toggle the grid snapping feature so that your drawings will automatically snap to grid lines in the background. This is very useful to easily draw tables, diagrams and lines.

Stickers

Save your frequently used symbols and diagrams, so you can easily drag and drop whenever you need them.

Laser

Scrivano offers a special laser tool to quickly draw over your notes. Incredibly useful when sharing your screen or presenting something!
